Do you get spam emails that are unwanted and often unsolicited, clogging your inbox with information from companies, services, or people you don't know or aren't interested in?

What are we building?

We are building a platform to solve the spam problem and make your email experience better which will:

  • Rewards you to receive and read emails
  • Limits your inbox only to important emails
  • Helps you to receive relevant mail only
  • Makes your email stands out in receivers' inbox

How it works?

It is a decentralized layer that works on top of any email service provider which works as follows:

The sender creates an email as usual; sender attaches crypto on the mail and creates a policy for redeeming it.

Before sending mail, the platform creates an email content hash; and saves it against the receiver address in a smart contract.

The recipient gets his crypto on the basis of the policy of the sender when the receiver receives the email.
