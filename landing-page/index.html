Octo-Enigma

What are we building?

We are building a way to make your email experience better which:

Will filter your mails,

Rewards you for opening mail, helping in preventing SPAM for you and others

Make email sender be it marketing channels, companies incentivise you to send you their emails

How it works?

It works on a decentralized layer on the top of email which adds cryto to sender which works as follows:

Before sending mail, it creates content hash and save it against the receiver in a smart contract and then it attaches a Crypto to the mail for receiver

Recipient gets its Crypto via smart contract when recipient opens and gets the mail.
